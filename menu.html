MENU

Durungawan is a progression of rare and beautiful ingredients where texture, flavour and harmony is paramount.

Continually inspired by nature, the creative process for chef Mark Lee begins in working closely with the farmers, fishermen, producers and artisans who cultivate bespoke produce exclusively for Quay. For each dish, the growth of every element and the selection of every ceramic piece is carefully considered and crafted for its role in the dining experience.

Durungawan brings a personal, interactive passage for diners through our culinary evolution.
