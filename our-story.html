Where the Idea Starts


Durungawan, a restaurant like no other. Our story began with a simple idea during a lunch with our team: to revolutionize the way we serve food in the Philippines. We wanted to showcase the incredible talent of our chefs and introduce a more profound degustation of Lechon, a traditional Filipino dish. And thus, the concept of molecular gastronomy was born. This sets us apart from other restaurants in the area. We are dedicated to exploring the beauty and richness of regional cuisines. We have gone on countless expeditions around the country, immersing ourselves in the culture and flavors of different regions. Our menu boasts the ingenuity, creativity, and wittiness of our local cuisine. We want our guests to experience a taste of the Philippines like never before.

Unique Feature


Our most popular and unique menu item is our Adobo as a plated dessert. It's a playful spin on a traditional dish that has become a favorite among our guests. We also offer a variety of dishes that showcase the beauty and richness of regional cuisines.


We are committed to having a positive impact on our local community and environment. We source our ingredients locally and reduce our environmental impact as much as possible. We want to promote our unique and interesting spin on Filipino dishes, locally and globally, to attract new customers and aid the progress of Durungawan.
Thank you for taking the time to learn about our story. We look forward to serving you and sharing our love for Filipino cuisine with you at Durungawan.
